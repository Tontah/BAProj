Moving Text Experiment

Mode

This is a camelCase vs under_score identifier experiment.

This experiment is constructed as follows;

Please read till the end

By clicking NEXT, you will go into the training mode

The page shows a sentence you need to study/memorize. Take your time for this.

Click on the identifier corresponding to the identifier you were presented with to study.

You will then be sent to the next page for the next identifier, and the cycle continues till the experiment ends

The time taken to click on the identifier in the moving text page will be recorded and the efficiency of the identifier clicked on will be noted to (i.e. if the identifier is right or not).

The training Mode last 30 rounds, the time for you to get acquainted with the experiment and the keyboard.

When you complete the training Mode, take some minutes to rest before pressing enter.

Pressing enter when you complete the training Mode takes you to the experiment.

The time begins to be recorded once the enter button is pressed.

A break will be proposed to you every 10 minutes, please take this break.

Thank you for your time and please be concentrated.

If there are any doubts, give me a call or leave me a message.

A download button will be shown at the end of the experiment, please downloas the document and send to me

You can write you critiques in a word document and send it alongside the excell document.

Have fun partaking in this experiment ☺
